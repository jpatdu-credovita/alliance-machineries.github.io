About Us

The Management

Arnaldo M. Saulong

Arnold hails from the province of Oriental Mindoro. He attended college at the Philippine Maritime Institute, majoring in Customs Administration. Starting his own business was always a dream of his. This dream was realised in the AMS Group of Companies; a closely held conglomerate of companies and ventures with various industries with operations in freight forwarding, customs brokerage, logistics & trucking, general trading, power generation, mining, manufacturing, manpower consulting, investments, education, and philanthropy.

His peers refer to him as a brilliant and highly educated leader, able to adapt and innovate throughout various industries. He is a passionate visionary known for his strategy and a committed, God-fearing servant of the Lord.

Arnold believes that technology and innovation is the main driving force of progress; in order to build an inclusive and equitable future, one must invest in the ingenuity and creativity of the Filipino people. This means uniting global best practices with local values and strategies. Overall, Arnold places his country and people first in his heart and mind, with the goal of leading the Philippines into the digital age.

“Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you'll land among the stars.” - Norman Peele

Noel Pangilinan

Noel comes from the province of Pampanga, one of the leading trading hubs in the country. Exposed to business and entrepreneurship at the young age of sixteen in the Angeles University Foundation, he qualified as the youngest official bidder of the United States Defense Reutilization and Marketing Service Asia Pacific. From there, he engaged in extensive trading between the US and Australia, bringing industrial, electrical and communications equipment to the Philippines.

In 1992, Noel ventured into the oil and gas industry, starting as the leading site coordinator in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. A fast and perceptive learner, he was instrumental in building the largest oil and gas separation plant in the world at the time.

After his career in the Middle East, he came home equipped with innovative solutions and formulas for fuel retail and distribution. Returning to his trading roots, Noel started Great Orient Trading, Pampanga’s leader in fuel dispenser distribution and gas station equipment importation.

Noel is the prime example of a person expert in global best practices coupled with classic Filipino values and strategies. Over the years, he has worked towards the progress of the Philippines and he strives to provide the best value in the most equitable way possible.

Dodie Liwanag

Jose Rodney, more commonly known as “Dodie”, is a 3rd-generation banker from Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro. His background is centred around economic development and inclusive financial management, both of which he majored in at the prestigious San Beda College; as manifested in his leadership in the rural banking sector. A proud Mindoreño, Dodie has always worked towards providing opportunities for his kababayan and building the capacity of his hometown. He has been the stalwart partner of Petron Corporation since 2000, bringing fuel and power to numerous enterprises across the province.

Aside from banking and petroleum, Dodie brings with him almost 20 years of combined experience from his other ventures in cable television, ice production, and management consulting. His sharp intellect and unique insights on sustainable development make him an asset for multinational companies who plan to enter the Philippine market. A seasoned and wise strategist, he is one of the key consultants at SMP&Co., a bespoke global finance, management, and technology strategy firm.

He currently serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of The Country Bank and he is actively involved with various local cooperative development initiatives both inside and outside the island province of Mindoro. He also serves as a Member of the Board of Trustees of the Easter Mindoro College as part of his passion for empowering the Filipino youth to take on the future.

Our History

Alliance Machineries started when Noel returned to the Philippines, with the goal of providing the Filipino entrepreneur with the best available technology for starting a fuel retail and distribution network in the country. The shared dream of Arnold, Noel, and Dodie to empower Filipinos to begin meaningful small enterprises has led to the creation of this company. With the combined expertise of these talented individuals, Alliance Machineries hopes to pioneer a new age of progression for all Filipinos.
